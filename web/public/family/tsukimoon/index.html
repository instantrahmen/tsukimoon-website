Introduction

Hey there! As you probably know by this point, my name is Tsukimoon, or Tsuki! I'm a green-themed loli Neko girl who's sole mission is to spread the message of positivity, friendship, and self love to others who need it. I'm the mother of a small, tight knit family of wonderful and versatile beans.. Hand picked by me, to join me in my cause! You'll often find me bouncing around the worlds of VRChat, being my typical goofy, cutesy self. I'm a mute, through and through, but I communicate using ASL! (American Sign Language) I'm still in the basic stages of learning, but I'm getting better every day!

History

Tsuki was created on December 25th, 2019. She began as a purple Neko, due to not having her own unique look yet. A month later, the black and green theme that became her permanant colours took shape in another small Neko avatar, made by a friend! She posted to her Twitter every single day from the day she created the account, doing her best to spread positive vibes and make people smile.

On January 23rd, 2019, Tsuki attempted her very first stream on Twitch. She recieved an overwhelming amount of love and support from the VRChat community, welcoming her into the fold with open arms! This gave her the confidence and determination to become a content creator, to further spread her message of wholesome fun.

On May 30th, 2019, Tsukimoon and BaniiKumaa announced to the world that they were dating, but they were dating in secret for a weeks before they said anything. They've been together for nearly 2 years now, creating content as a couple!
