Introduction

Hiyaaa, I'm Baby! I'm a Bunny/Bear Hybrid, hence my unique alias "BaniiKuma" (Bunny Bear in Japanese). I'm a lover of apples, pastel colors, music, dance, and content creation! My goal is to help those who feel excluded, or outcasted or sad, or just plain lonely, feel like they have a place to go to where they fit in, and so they have a community of others who they can talk to and relate to. I've always had a mission of making people happy, spreading positivity, and giving smiles to those who are in need of it. I don't care for the views, or the relevancy, or any of that.. I care for the people! The main thing you'll see me streaming is VRChat, where I sit and converse and world hop with my friends in game, as well as in chat! But I am a Variety Vtuber, so once in a while I'll branch out to other things. In my streams, you'll see me just being my silly, degenerate, goofy self, and I hope you can be a degenerate with me! Much loves, cuties!

History Baby started in VRChat on May 1st 2018. She's always wanted to become a Vtuber ever since discovering them after being sad for a long time. She became one in May 2020. She was and still is very socially awkward and shy, so she didn't think she would ever actually have the courage to stream. But one day she let go of her worries, and tried it out. And she got an overwhelming amount of love and support from her friends! Ever since that first stream, she's been amazed by how much fun one can have, just sitting down, talking and getting to know people. And each time she streams, her goal is always the same. To make others happy, and help them feel included and wanted and apart of something. She wants to spread joy, and happiness to others, just by being herself. And so far, it seems to have been working really well for her. She will continue doing what she loves, not only for herself, but for others as well.
